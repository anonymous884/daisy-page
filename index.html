Daisy-TTS

Simulating Wider Spectrum of Emotions via Prosody Embedding Decomposition.

We introduce Daisy-TTS, and emotional text-to-speech design, grounded on the structural model of emotions, to simulate wide spectrum of emotion through learning emotionally-separable prosody embeddings.

Through learning and decomposing the learned embeddings, Daisy-TTS capable of simulating:

  • Primary Emotions, as learned from the training samples, e.g. joy, sadness, anger.
  • Secondary Emotions, as a mixture of primary emotions, e.g. envy = sadness + anger.
  • Intensity-level, by scaling the emotion embedding, e.g. rage = 2 * anger.
  • Emotions Polarity, by negating the emotion embedding, e.g. sadness = - joy.

Emotion Synthesis

To control the learned prosody embeddings, we perform eigen decomposition to the embeddings (similar to Eigenfaces and Eigenvoices). We found that we can synthesize Primary and Mixed emotions from the weighted linear combination of the first 5 eigenvalues and vectors of the embeddings.
