Daisy-TTS

Simulating Mixed Emotions with Prosody Latent Representation.

Daisy-TTS is a prosody Prosody refers to the rhythm, stress, and intonation of speech. -controllable text-to-speech model that simulates the structural model of emotions. We learn emotionally-clustered prosody embeddings and manipulate them to produce synthetic emotions.

Following the structural model of emotions, Daisy can:

  • Combine embeddings of primary emotions to produce mixed emotions. For example, 'Envy' is synthesized from the embeddings of 'Anger' + 'Sadness'.
  • Adjust the intensity of an emotion by manipulating the embedding's magnitude!
  • Create a polar emotion by negating the embedding. The polar opposite of Sad is Joy!

Emotion Synthesis

To control the learned prosody embeddings, we perform eigen decomposition to the embeddings (similar to Eigenfaces and Eigenvoices). We found that we can synthesize Primary and Mixed emotions from the weighted linear combination of the first 5 eigenvalues and vectors of the embeddings.
